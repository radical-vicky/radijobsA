General

What is RadiloxRemoteJobs?
RadiloxRemoteJobs is a remote IT talent platform that connects administrators with technical professionals through project-based hiring. Candidates complete real projects from job ads and present them live via Zoom.

How does the subscription work?
Freelancers pay a monthly subscription fee to access the platform. There are no commissions on tasks - you keep 100% of what you earn. You can cancel anytime.

What payment methods are accepted?
We support multiple payment methods including cryptocurrency (Binance Pay, OKX Pay), PayPal, M-Pesa, and bank transfers.

How do I get hired?
Browse jobs, apply with your profile, take the technical quiz, build the project from the job ad, present it live via Zoom, and get hired based on your demonstrated skills.

Is there a free trial?
Yes! You can browse jobs and view details for free. Subscribe to apply to jobs and start earning.

For Freelancers

How much does it cost to join?
Monthly subscription is $19/month. There are NO commissions on tasks - you keep 100% of what you earn.

When do I get paid?
Once your task is approved by the admin, the budget amount is added to your wallet immediately. You can withdraw anytime.

What are the withdrawal fees?
Withdrawal fee is 3% of the withdrawal amount. Minimum withdrawal is $20, maximum is $5,000 per transaction.

Can I work multiple jobs simultaneously?
Yes! Unlike other platforms, you can work unlimited jobs simultaneously. Maximize your earning potential.

For Employers

How do I post a job?
Log in as an admin and navigate to the Jobs section. Click "Create New Job" and fill out the job details including the project brief that candidates must complete.

What is a project brief?
The project brief is a real project that candidates must build and present during their Zoom interview. This verifies their actual skills before hiring.

How does the interview process work?
There are two Zoom meetings: First is the interview (60 min) where candidates present their project. Second is onboarding (45 min) where we train on platform usage.

Payments

What cryptocurrencies are accepted?
We accept USDT and BUSD via Binance Pay, and USDT/USDC via OKX Pay for both subscriptions and withdrawals.

How long do withdrawals take?
Crypto and PayPal withdrawals are instant after admin approval. M-Pesa takes a few minutes. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days.

Is my payment information secure?
Yes! All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways. We never store your full payment details on our servers.

Technical

What browsers are supported?
We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.

Is there a mobile app?
The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are coming soon!

How do I reset my password?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
