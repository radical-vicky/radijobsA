Our Story

Founded in 2026, RadiloxRemoteJobs was built to solve the growing problem of resume fraud and hiring friction in remote IT recruiting. Traditional platforms rely on self-reported skills and credentials that are easy to fake, leading to wasted time, money, and opportunity.

We created a platform where candidates prove their abilities by building real projects from actual job ads and presenting their work live via Zoom. This eliminates guesswork and ensures that hiring decisions are based on demonstrated skills, not polished resumes.

Today, we're proud to connect thousands of vetted IT professionals with forward-thinking companies worldwide. With zero commission on tasks and multiple payment options, we're building a fairer, more accessible future for remote work.
